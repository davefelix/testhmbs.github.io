Students

Welcome to the Student Portal!

This portal is designed to help students easily access their academic records and complete school registration online. By entering your school ID number and full name, you can view your class grades in a structured and organized format.

Additionally, if you are a student in Grade 10, Grade 11, or Grade 12, you can register for school online without needing to visit the school in person. This makes the registration process faster and more convenient.

Both Students portals are not available now.

    Important Notes:

  • Grade 9 students are currently not eligible for online registration.
  • If you do not know your school ID number , you can get it from the school principal. The school ID number is provided to all students and is required for both grade checking and online registration.
  • Make sure to enter your details correctly to avoid any issues.
  • The grade viewing and online registration features will only be available when the school notifies students through the official school Telegram channel or announces it on the school bulletin board.

If you experience any problems, please contact the school administration for assistance.
